Vision

With an emphasis on medical and health science, Dr. Sammy Lee Medical and Health Science Magnet will develop students who can succeed to their highest potential to become critical thinkers in a diverse and global society.



Mission

Dr. Sammy Lee Medical and Health Science Magnet will empower students with a high-quality collaborative learning environment with an emphasis on medicine and health science that supports the development of life long learners
